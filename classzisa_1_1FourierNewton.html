Detailed Description

Find Gauss-Legendre quadrature point by root finding.

The quadrature points of Gauss-Legendre are the roots of the Legendre polynomials.

This algorithm utilized the fact the roots of the Fourier series of the Legendre-polynomial are nearly evenly spaced. Hence, a good initial guess for Newton's method exists and the roots are found using Newtons method.

References: [0]: Belousov, Legendre Polynomials: Mathematical Tables Series, 1962. [1]: Swarztrauber, SIAM J. Sci. Comp. 2002 [2]: Hale, Townsend, SIAM J. Sci. Comp. 2013
