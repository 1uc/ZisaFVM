Detailed Description

Performs visualization only on a subset of ranks.

The idea is to gather the data on specific ranks, and then run the desired visualization on a separate thread on those ranks.

The logic of gathering the data is abstracted in ArrayGatherer. The logic for visualization is abstracted in Visualization.
